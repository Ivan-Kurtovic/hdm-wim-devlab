  • Functions in the toolbar from left to right: leave the session, view session information, open Google Drive, and open help.
    The graphic in the center of the screen shows you all available documents. Documents, which are considered as relevant for the current conversation, are highlighted. By clicking on the sector of a document you can open it. It will open in a new tab, which you can navigate in by closing the current tab or changing the selected tab by clicking on a different one in the tab bar.
